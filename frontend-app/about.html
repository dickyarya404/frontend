About Us

History of Deus Express

Deus Xpress is a logistic service with an extensive ecosystem throughout Indonesia. We aim to create better job opportunities to support Indonesia's economy. Started in 2018, Deus Xpress has now become one of the fastest growing logistics companies in Indonesia that adapts and embraces the technology at the core of our services.

Consistently fast, responsible and reliable service has brought Deus Xpress into a higher credibility for customers and partners. Along with the increase in foreign investment, national economic growth, the information technology development, and innovations of developed products, the performance of Deus Xpress keeps growing and developing in the business world and the people of Indonesia.

The business growth and lifestyle evolution has contributed in increasing time-sensitive import delivery demand. Not only small package and document scope, but also in handling transportation, logistics, and distribution. This growing opportunity has encouraged Deus Xpress to expand its network to all major cities in Indonesia. Today, JNE has reached over 6,000 service locations and still counting, with more than 40,000 employees.

More than 150 Deus Xpress service locations has been linked with online communication system, guided by the system and information site access that is effective and efficient for consumers to trace and track the current status of packages or documents delivery. Deus Xpress incorporates both human resources and technology such as X-Ray machine, GPS, and satellite communication tools at the same time as a part of the company development.
