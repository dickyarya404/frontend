Frequently Asked Questions

What are the advantages of Deus Express?

You can easily find out the shipping tariff, and check the nearest drop point (branch office) to your location through the application and website. The facilities trace & tracking system will make you easier to track your packages' location during the shipping process. Deus Express Drop points operate every day (including weekends, and National Holidays)

How is Deus Express payment system?

For non-corporate customers, the payments should be in cash when sending the package through drop point. For corporate customers, the payment system can be made with special terms and conditions.

How do you check Deus Express waybill number?

You can check Deus Express waybill number on Deus Express website www.jet.co.id or mobile application.

How and when do you get Deus Express waybill number?

Waybill numbers will be given directly by Deus Express when the package is sent via drop point.

How do you proceed a claim?

For proceed the claim, you can contact J&T Express call center 021-8066-1888. Then you can take the claim form at the nearest drop point and complete all the needed requirements.

How to send a package with insurance service?

You can tell the Deus Express drop point admin that you want to insure your shipment goods. Then, Deus Express will calculate your package insurance costs with a calculation 0.2% from the price of the invoice.
