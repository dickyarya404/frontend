Guidance

How to pack liquids and fragile goods

Make sure the goods are packed by using cardboard box, Cover the goods by using bubble wrap,use at least three to five layers of bubble wrap depends on the fragility of the package, Use PE foam on each side of the box for a shock absorber, Make sure the size of the goods is appropriate with the box

How to pack electronic goods

Every goods must have an outer packaging or commercial packaging, Cover goods by using bubble wrap, Use PE foam on each side of the box for a shock absorber, Make sure the size of the goods is appropriate with the box

How to pack large and long goods

Every goods must have an outer packaging or commercial packaging, Cover goods by using bubble wrap, use at least three to five layers of bubble wrap depends on he fragility of he package, Sealed the box by using duct tape tightly to avoid shocks that can cause damages

How to attach shipping label

If the sender attaches a shipping label from e-commerce platfrorm, Make sure is attached to a flat surface, not folded and not bubbling

How to attach the duct tape

The box needs to be sealed by duct tape, Make "#" form, Make sure the duct tape is sealed tightly and not bubbling

Package size information

Size of the package must be bigger than Deus Express electronik receipt (25 cm x 15 cm)

Dangerous Goods

Dangerous Goods Class 1

Fireworks, Ammunition/cartridges, Airbag inflators, TNT compositions, Flares

Dangerous Goods Class 2

Aerosol, Gas Cylinder (filled), Silicon, Fire Stop, Nail Polish, Gas CO2

Dangerous Goods Class 3

Glue, Parfume, Paint Cans, Oil, Sanpoly, Toner, Gasoline gauge

Dangerous Goods Class 4

Alkali metals, Alumunium phosephide, Carbon, Camphor, Oily fabrics

Dangerous Goods Class 5

Organic peroxides, Oxidizing substances

Dangerous Goods Class 5

Pesticide, Toner Powder, Insecticide

Prohibited

Firearms

All kinds of ammunitions, firearms, bullets, grenades, bombs, etc

Explosive Materials

Easily explosive materials like explosive materials & detonator, gunpowder, firecrackers, etc

Flammable

All kinds of flammable material, including liquid, gas and solid chemistry. Like gasoline, kerosene, alcohol, varnish, diesel fuel, aerosol/ spray tube, lighters, gas holder, phosphorus, sulfur, matches, etc

Drugs

All kinds of narcotic drugs like, opium (including flowers, sprout and opium leaf), morn, cocaine, heroin, marijuana, methamphetamine, ephedrine, and other related products

Chemical

All kinds of corrosive materials like sulfuric acid, hydrochloric acid, nitric acid, organic solvents, pesticide, hydrogen peroxide, and other hazardous chemicals

Radioactive

Various types of radioactive elements and its depository like uranium, cobalt, radium and plutonium
